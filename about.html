Our Story

Catie and Kyle met back in June of 2012. Kyle had just graduated from the Art Institute of Jacksonville with Web Design and Interactive Media, around the same time his father retired from the Coast Gaurd.

Catie had just finished her Sophomore year at the Savannah College of Art and Design with her focus in Jewelry. Funny enough, they met via OKCupid (online dating of all things!) because they were looking for relationships outside of what they were getting. Catie messaged Kyle first saying that they should be friends and they instantly hit it off.

They chatted online for a few days, after which Kyle drove up for the weekend to visit and meet her face to face.

The rest is history...
