Our Story

We met back around June 16, 2012. This was right after Kyle graduated from the Art Institute of Jacksonville, FL. & around the same time his father retired from the Coast Gaurd.

Funny enough Catie and Kyle met via OKCupid of all things... Online dating, how about that? Catie messaged Kyle first saying that we should be friends. She lived in Savannah, GA at the time, and myself in St. Marys, GA. Roughly 2hrs away from each other.

We chatted online for a few days, after which I drove up for the weekend to visit and meet her face to face.

The rest is history...

What was Catie up to back then?

Catie was going to school in Savannah at Savannah College of Art and Design, (SCAD) for Jewelery and Objects. Kyle found this to be a very unusual field, and is constantly fascinated by the work his lady does.
